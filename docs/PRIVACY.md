# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 3, 2026  
**Last Updated**: January 3, 2026

## Introduction

WithYou is a private, relationship communication app designed to help couples plan intentional connection time. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

**Beta Notice**: WithYou is currently in beta testing. This privacy policy applies to the beta version and will be updated before public release.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use WithYou, you provide us with:

- **Account Information**: Email address and encrypted password
- **Relationship Data**: Pairing status with your partner
- **Check-ins**: Mood levels (1-5 scale) and optional notes you choose to share
- **Preferences**: Activity styles, food preferences, budget levels, and energy preferences
- **Saved Ideas**: Date ideas you choose to save

### Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Usage Information**: Features used, screens visited, app crash reports
- **Log Data**: API requests, timestamps, error logs (stored temporarily for debugging)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Location data or GPS coordinates
- Contact lists or address books
- Photos or media files
- Messages or conversations outside the app
- Social media information
- Advertising identifiers or tracking pixels

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Service**: Enable pairing, check-ins, preferences, and idea generation
2. **Improve the App**: Fix bugs, analyze usage patterns, and develop new features
3. **Communicate with You**: Send important updates about the service (no marketing emails)
4. **Ensure Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- **Database**: PostgreSQL database hosted on secure servers
- **Encryption**: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry-standard one-way encryption)
- **Session Tokens**: JWT tokens with 7-day expiration for authentication
- **Mobile Storage**: Session tokens stored securely using expo-secure-store (encrypted on-device)

### Security Measures

We implement security best practices:

- HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
- Password hashing with bcrypt (cannot be reversed)
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Input validation to prevent injection attacks
- Regular security updates and patches

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained as long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: After 12 months of inactivity, accounts may be archived or deleted
- **Deleted Accounts**: When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
- **Backup Data**: Backups are retained for 90 days for disaster recovery, then permanently deleted

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Limited Sharing

We only share data in these specific circumstances:

1. **With Your Partner**: When you choose to share a check-in, your partner can see your mood level and timestamp
2. **Service Providers**: Trusted infrastructure providers (database hosting, app deployment) who are contractually obligated to protect your data
3. **Legal Compliance**: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
4. **Safety**: To prevent harm, fraud, or illegal activity

### Analytics and Tracking

- **Current Beta**: We do not use analytics or tracking services
- **Future Consideration**: If analytics are added, we will use privacy-respecting tools and update this policy

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
- View all data associated with your account within the app
- Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format (email contact below)

### Delete Your Data
- Delete your account at any time from Settings
- All personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
- Partner relationship data is also deleted (your partner will be unpaired)

### Control Sharing
- Choose whether to share each check-in with your partner (default: not shared)
- End your pairing relationship at any time
- Update or delete preferences at any time

### Opt-Out
- No marketing emails (we don't send them)
- No push notifications in current beta version

## Children's Privacy

WithYou is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and data immediately.

## International Users

WithYou is operated in the United States. If you are located outside the U.S., your data will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using WithYou, you consent to this transfer.

**GDPR Notice**: If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR, including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as WithYou evolves. When we make changes:

- For material changes, we will notify you via email or in-app notice
- Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance

- Notify you within 72 hours via email
- Explain what data was affected
- Describe steps we are taking to resolve the issue
- Provide guidance on protecting your account
