Core Features

Built for Trust

Everything you need to communicate intentionally and connect with purpose.

Private by Design

Your data is encrypted, never shared, and never monetized. One partner per account. Full control over what you share.

Consent-Based Pairing

Invite codes ensure both partners opt-in. No surveillance, no tracking, no pressure. Either partner can end pairing anytime.

Indirect Signals

Share moods, preferences, and availability without direct pressure. Let data guide conversation, not demand it.

Smart Preferences

Set activity styles, food preferences, budget, and energy levels. Get date ideas that actually match both of you.

Check-In Moods

Log your mood (1-5) and choose whether to share with your partner. No pressure to explain your feelings.

Date Ideas

Receive curated date ideas based on your shared preferences. Save favorites and plan intentional time together.

Process

How It Works

Simple steps to intentional connection.

1

Create & Pair

Sign up, create an account, and generate an invite code. Share it with your partner—they confirm, and you're paired.

2

Set Preferences

Choose activity styles, food preferences, budget levels, and energy levels. This guides future date ideas.

3

Check In Together

Log your mood (1-5) and choose whether to share with your partner. No pressure to explain.

4

Get Ideas & Plan

Receive date ideas based on your mood, preferences, and availability. Save favorites and plan intentional time.

5

Grow Together

Track your patterns, celebrate shared moments, and discover what actually works for you as a couple.
